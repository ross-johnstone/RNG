About RNG


RNG is a collaborative video game database website that allows anyone to search and sort Relatively New Games (released in the past 10 years) and view what users and verified critics have rated these games. If you sign up and sign in you can join in on the action and Rate New Games too! Be sure to use our categories function to help find similar games and be on the lookout for a critic tag on a user as that means that the user is one of our verified critics.
